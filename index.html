Frequently Asked Questions

Get Answers About Water Solutions

We drill a variety of boreholes, including domestic, agricultural, and industrial. Our team is experienced in drilling different depths and geological formations across Gauteng to ensure the most effective water supply for your specific needs.

We offer a wide range of water purification systems, from basic sediment filtration to advanced reverse osmosis and UV sterilization. We conduct a water quality test to help you choose the right system for your needs.

If you have concerns about the quality of your borehole or municipal water, or if you experience issues like taste, odour, or discoloration, a water test can determine the contaminants. A purification system can then be installed to ensure clean and safe water.

JoJo tanks provide reliable water storage, helping you manage water supply during municipal outages or periods of high demand. They are essential for storing borehole water and also offer a sustainable solution for rainwater harvesting, reducing your reliance on municipal water.

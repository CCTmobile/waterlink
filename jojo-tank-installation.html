How we approach it

Our Installation Approach

We don't just place a tank; we design a complete water storage system. This includes proper base preparation, seamless integration with your existing plumbing or borehole system, and the installation of high-quality booster pumps for optimal pressure.

Base Construction

Constructing solid, level concrete bases to support the weight of full tanks.

Automated Filling

Installation of float valves and sensors for automatic tank filling from any source.

Booster Pumps

Integration of energy-efficient booster pumps to ensure consistent water pressure.

Custom Plumbing

Expert pipe-laying and connections using durable, UV-resistant materials.

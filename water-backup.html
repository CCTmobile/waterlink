Reliable Municipal Water Backup for Gauteng Homeowners

Water outages and pressure failures are becoming more common in Gauteng. Our municipal water backup systems are designed to provide you with peace of mind. We install storage tanks (JoJo) that automatically fill from your municipal line, ensuring a steady supply of water during any outage or period of low pressure.

How we approach it

Our Seamless Backup Solution

Our systems are set up with bypass valves and high-quality booster pumps, so you'll never even notice when the municipal water goes off. The storage tank sits at the ready, and when the supply is cut, your system automatically switches to your stored reserve.

Automatic Filling

Float valves ensure your JoJo tank stays full and ready to go whenever it's needed.

Bypass Integration

Seamless plumbing integration that allows your home to run on either municipal or tank water.

High-Pressure Booster

Integrated booster pumps ensure your home maintains constant high water pressure at all times.

Certified Installations

We align with professional standards to guarantee a reliable and long-lasting installation.

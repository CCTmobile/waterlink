How we approach it

Advanced Siting Technology

We employ a variety of electronic surveying methods, including resistivity and electromagnetic profiling, to map the underground geological structures. This allows us to identify fractures, aquifers, and water-bearing zones with high precision.

Geophysical Mapping

Using electronic tools to "see" underground before drilling starts.

Risk Reduction
